Mainstream Inclusive Green Economy (IGE) Principles in the Urban Sector in India
The course provides a comprehensive understanding of Inclusive Green Economy (IGE) principles, as well as the existing and enabling legal, policy, and regulatory frameworks along with the strategic master planning process. It aims to equip learners with the knowledge and tools necessary to effectively support and implement IGE principles across various sectors and levels of government. Additionally, actionable strategies for addressing sector-specific challenges and concerns are incorporated. The course is divided into two set of modules: Module A: Strategic Sessions Session A01: Introduction: Why the transition to IGE is urgent and important Session A02: Existing and enabling institutional arrangements for the IGE transition Session A03: Implementing IGE Principles Module B: Thematic Sessions Session B01: Solid Waste Management Session B02: Wastewater Management Session B03: Blue-Green Infrastructure: Natural and Built environments Session B04: Sustainable Mobility Session B05: Air Quality Session B06: Climate Change for Course
