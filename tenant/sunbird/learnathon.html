अrban Learnathon 2.0

The National Urban Learning Platform is taking a leap with the launch of one of India’s biggest urban learning challenge, “The Urban Learnathon”. The objective of the Learnathon is to recognize and celebrate the best practices and innovations in the form of urban solutions, as well as foster collaborative learning. The tagline for the Urban Learnathon is simple, “Create Collaborate Celebrate” which defines the essence of NULP --- a) Create user-generated content b) Solve urban challenges together and c) Get recognized as NULP Champions.
At the end of the Learnathon, NULP will have best urban solutions and ideas from Municipal employees, smart city employees, state employees which ultimately redefines peer-to-peer learning. This 45-day long pan India challenge will be entirely hosted on the NULP web portal, where the participants can send entries in 12 regional languages including English and Hindi under diverse themes.
